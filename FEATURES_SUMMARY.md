## ⚠️ Technical Limitations (Streamlit Framework)

### What's NOT Possible in Streamlit:

1. **Real-time Animated Avatar with Lip-Sync**
   - ❌ Streamlit doesn't support WebSocket/WebRTC
   - ❌ No real-time 3D rendering
   - ❌ No lip-sync libraries available
   - **Alternative**: Static avatar with animations (what we have)

2. **Persistent Floating Elements**
   - ❌ Fixed position elements don't persist across page changes
   - ❌ Streamlit rerenders entire page on interaction
   - **Alternative**: Chatbot at bottom of each page

3. **Continuous Voice Conversation**
   - ❌ No real-time audio streaming
   - ❌ Voice plays once per interaction
   - **Alternative**: Voice plays on page load and responses
